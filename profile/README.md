# [Bridging Seas](https://www.bridgingseas.com/)

## What is Bridging Seas?
Bridging Seas is a youth-led global organization that aims to unify the Asian diaspora. Through our international **pen pal program**, we connect high school students across countries to build lasting friendships and learn from one another's cultures and lived experiences.

Our goal is to create a platform where high school students can learn about one another's cultures, while giving members of the Asian diaspora a deeper understanding of how cultures evolve across different parts of the world. We envision a more compassionate and understanding world where students embrace their roots and appreciate different ways of life through meaningful connections with international friends.

### Our Impact
Our program has impacted **400+ pen pals in 12 countries**. We have **20+ chapters across 17 countries**. 

## Get Involved
### [Join the Pen Pal Program](https://bridgingseas.carrd.co/#penpalprograms)

- **Diaspora Exchange** is a pen pal program that specifically provides spaces for students of the same ethnicity, and who live in different countries to become pen pals. This program aims to help students of diasporic communities to understand their cultural roots better and appreciate how their culture changes and adapts in various regions

- **Open Seas** is a global pen pal program that connects students across different ethnic, cultural, and national backgrounds. Open Seas is designed to foster cross-cultural understanding by pairing students with peers whose lived experiences, traditions, and perspectives may differ from their own.
